About Me

I'm Gene Kristian Z. Salvador, a Drafting Technology graduate with strong proficiency in CAD, BIM, and 3D modeling software.
I specialize in architectural drafting, design presentation, and photo and video editing using tools like AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, Adobe Creative Suite, and Blender.
I’ve gained hands-on experience through my internship at JR Contractor, where I assisted in architectural and interior design projects,
and currently work as a Multimedia Artist at Virac Town Center, creating visual content for promotions and events.
With a National Certificate II in Architectural Drafting and a passion for design and detail, I’m committed to delivering high-quality visual and technical outputs in both architectural and multimedia fields.
